# Zen Keeper: Sophia's Light

> *"They are not exactly enemies. They are ancient programs doing exactly what they were designed to do- keep you fully immersed in the system. Your only job is to notice them."*

---

## What Is This

Zen Keeper is a Gnostic-themed meditation trainer and awareness tracker. It is built on the practice of thought-watching — observing the mind without being captured by it.

The mythology: **Sophia**, divine wisdom, was fragmented and scattered into matter by the Archons. Every moment of pure awareness you hold returns a fragment of her light. Your orb is your piece of that light. It grows brighter, larger, and more complex with accumulated practice.

The Archons are the twelve programs that pull attention away from the present moment — not villains, but ancient broadcast signals doing exactly what they were designed to do. Noticing them is the practice. Noticing them IS the light returning.

---

## How To Play

1. Tap **Begin Session** on the home screen
2. Tap the orb to start a timed round (10 seconds to start)
3. Watch the orb. Try to simply be aware of awareness itself
4. When the singing bowl dings, the round is complete
5. Choose: **Clarity Held** (no Archon visited) or scroll through the carousel to identify which Archon pulled your attention
6. Repeat through up to 7 rounds per session (10 → 20 → 30 → 45 → 60 → 90 → 120 seconds)
7. End your session anytime — all progress counts
8. Record any **downloads** (insights, transmissions) that arose during the session
9. Return. The orb remembers.

Your orb evolves through 10 levels as you accumulate light:

| Level | Name | Color |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Flickering Ember | Pale grey |
| 2 | Seeker | Warm amber |
| 3 | Watcher | Gold |
| 4 | Light Keeper | Deep gold |
| 5 | Thread Weaver | Rose |
| 6 | Archon Witness | Sky blue |
| 7 | Sophia's Heir | Violet |
| 8 | Flame Carrier | Deep violet |
| 9 | Cosmic Weaver | Iridescent |
| 10 | Living Light | White-gold |

**Light earned per round:**
- Clarity round: round duration × 2
- Archon caught: round duration × 1
- Both are wins. Catching an Archon IS awareness.

## Features

- Timed rounds with a visual ring timer around the orb
- Singing bowl tone on round completion (Web Audio API)
- Archon carousel picker — swipe or tap arrows, full illustration + description + quote per card
- Persistent orb progression saved to browser local storage
- Nemesis tracker — your most frequent Archon visitor
- Sophia Fragments journal — searchable record of insights and downloads
- Archon Bestiary with visit counts per Archon
- Animated starfield, floating orb, pulsing glow rings
- Full dark space aesthetic, no harsh elements


---

## Data & Privacy

All data is stored locally in your browser via `localStorage`. Nothing is sent anywhere. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. If you clear your browser cache, your orb resets. To preserve your progress across devices, a developer can add cloud sync (Supabase free tier works well for this).
